Human Trafficking in Utah

On this website you'll find information about human trafficking and how you can help stop it here in the state of Utah. Check out the Learn More page to see this information or go to the Data page to see a list of missing people we suspect are being trafficked.

Human Trafficking - a crime of exploitation of someone for the purposes of compelled labor or a commercial sex act.

There are two types of Trafficking-

  • Sex Trafficking: modern-day slavery where individuals perform commercial sex through the use of force, fraud, or coercion.
  • Labor Trafficking: modern-day slavery where individuals perform labor or services through the use of force, fraud, or coercion.

Human trafficking is a serious issue that exists throughout all of Utah as well as the rest of the nation. It is important to be informed about human trafficking so that you can help protect others as well as alert authorities in the case that you have any information that could save lives.
